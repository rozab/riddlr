Simple Guide to becoming a Riddlr

Writing a riddle is the reverse of solving a puzzle – you have to start with the answer.
So first, choose something to write about (objects or animals are good for beginners).

Once you have your answer you can begin to think of some clues that relate to your answer.
Sentences such as:
   - I look like…
   - I sound like…
   - You find me…
   - I have…
   - I am…
   - I feel…

Give it a go!
