Our Mission

Riddlr is a revolutionary social platform which aims to pull the ancient art of riddle-telling into the 21st century. Our use of sans-serif fonts looks to the future while our minimalist design and gentle colour palette harkens back to the riddle's ancient Greek heritage.

Riddlr was developed by Rory Barnett, Christopher Gregory, Marc Herbert and Faizan Mohamed.
