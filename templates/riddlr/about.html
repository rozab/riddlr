About

Riddlr is an online riddle sharing site for riddle solvers of all ages. Users can find riddles to solve straight from the home page, sorted in categories such as: newly created or top rated riddles, and upvote or downvote each riddle solved. User specific riddles can also be found on that userâ€™s profile page and each user has an associated karma - the sum of the ratings of all their riddles. Additionally, users have a score calculated from the number of riddles they have solved. A difficulty will also be assigned to each riddle. This is calculated from how many guesses users take to solve the riddle (with an initial value supplied upon creation). Solving difficult riddles quickly will raise a users score faster.
